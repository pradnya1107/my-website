Yoga Classes

Find Balance, Flexibility, and Inner Peace

Transform Your Mind and Body

Yoga is an ancient practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to promote physical and mental well-being. Our expert instructors guide you through various yoga styles, helping you build flexibility, strength, and mindfulness in a peaceful environment.

Types of Yoga We Offer

Hatha Yoga

Beginner Friendly 60 minutes

Hatha yoga is perfect for beginners. It focuses on basic postures and breathing techniques, moving at a slower pace to help you build strength and flexibility.

What to Expect:

  • Basic yoga poses and stretches
  • Breathing exercises (pranayama)
  • Relaxation and meditation
  • Focus on proper alignment
  • Gentle, slow-paced movements

Benefits:

  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Strengthens core muscles
  • Improves posture
  • Enhances mental clarity
Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday - 7:00 AM & 6:00 PM

Vinyasa Flow Yoga

Intermediate 75 minutes

Vinyasa yoga features flowing movements synchronized with breath. It's a dynamic practice that builds strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance.

What to Expect:

  • Flowing sequences of poses
  • Breath-synchronized movement
  • Sun salutations
  • Creative transitions between poses
  • Increased heart rate and calorie burn

Benefits:

  • Builds cardiovascular strength
  • Improves coordination and balance
  • Increases muscle tone
  • Enhances mental focus
  • Burns calories effectively
Schedule: Tuesday, Thursday - 7:00 AM & 7:00 PM

Yin Yoga

Beginner Friendly 60 minutes

Yin yoga is a slow-paced style with passive poses held for longer periods (3-5 minutes). It targets deep connective tissues and promotes relaxation.

What to Expect:

  • Passive poses held 3-5 minutes
  • Floor-based positions
  • Minimal muscle engagement
  • Meditation and mindfulness
  • Gentle stretching and release

Benefits:

  • Releases deep tension
  • Improves joint mobility
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances flexibility
  • Promotes relaxation and sleep
Schedule: Sunday - 10:00 AM & 5:00 PM

Power Yoga

Advanced 60 minutes

Power yoga is an intense, fitness-based approach to vinyasa-style yoga. It builds strength, stamina, and flexibility through challenging sequences.

What to Expect:

  • Fast-paced, dynamic sequences
  • Strength-building poses
  • High-intensity workout
  • Minimal rest between poses
  • Full-body conditioning

Benefits:

  • Builds muscle strength
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Enhances flexibility and balance
  • Burns significant calories
  • Increases endurance
Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday - 6:00 PM

Better Sleep

Helps combat insomnia and improves sleep quality
