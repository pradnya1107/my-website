Build Strength, Gain Muscle, Transform Your Body

Master Weight Training

Weight training is one of the most effective ways to build muscle mass, increase strength, and improve overall body composition. Our state-of-the-art equipment and expert guidance will help you achieve your fitness goals safely and effectively.

Our Weight Training Equipment

Barbell Bench Press

Intermediate

Muscles Targeted:

  • Pectoralis Major (Chest)
  • Anterior Deltoids (Front Shoulders)
  • Triceps

How to Use:

  1. Lie flat on the bench with your feet firmly on the floor
  2. Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width
  3. Lower the bar to your chest in a controlled manner
  4. Press the bar up until your arms are fully extended
  5. Repeat for 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

Squat Rack

Intermediate

Muscles Targeted:

  • Quadriceps (Front Thighs)
  • Glutes (Buttocks)
  • Hamstrings (Back Thighs)
  • Core

How to Use:

  1. Position the barbell at upper chest/shoulder height
  2. Step under the bar and rest it on your upper back
  3. Lift the bar off the rack and step back
  4. Lower your body by bending knees and hips
  5. Descend until thighs are parallel to the floor
  6. Drive through your heels to return to standing
  7. Complete 3-4 sets of 8-10 reps
ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Keep your knees aligned with your toes and maintain a straight back throughout the movement.

Lat Pulldown Machine

Beginner

Muscles Targeted:

  • Latissimus Dorsi (Back)
  • Rhomboids
  • Biceps
  • Rear Deltoids

How to Use:

  1. Sit at the machine with thighs secured under pads
  2. Grip the bar wider than shoulder-width
  3. Pull the bar down to your upper chest
  4. Squeeze your back muscles at the bottom
  5. Slowly return the bar to starting position
  6. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps
ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Lean back slightly and pull with your back muscles, not just your arms.

Leg Press Machine

Beginner

Muscles Targeted:

  • Quadriceps
  • Glutes
  • Hamstrings
  • Calves

How to Use:

  1. Sit in the machine with your back flat against the pad
  2. Place feet shoulder-width apart on the platform
  3. Lower the weight by bending your knees
  4. Lower until knees form a 90-degree angle
  5. Press the platform away with your heels
  6. Extend legs fully without locking knees
  7. Do 3-4 sets of 12-15 reps
ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Keep your knees aligned and never lock your knees at the top of the movement.

Cable Crossover Machine

Intermediate

Muscles Targeted:

  • Pectoralis Major
  • Anterior Deltoids
  • Triceps

How to Use:

  1. Set pulleys to upper position and select weight
  2. Stand in the center with one foot forward
  3. Grab handles with arms slightly bent
  4. Pull handles together in front of your chest
  5. Squeeze chest muscles at the peak
  6. Slowly return to starting position
  7. Complete 3 sets of 12-15 reps
ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Maintain a slight forward lean and focus on squeezing your chest muscles together.

Smith Machine

Beginner

Muscles Targeted:

  • Full Body (depending on exercise)
  • Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings
  • Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
  • Back and Biceps

How to Use:

  1. The Smith Machine is versatile for multiple exercises
  2. Rotate the bar to unlock it from the safety hooks
  3. Perform your exercise (squats, bench press, shoulder press)
  4. Rotate the bar back to lock it when finished
  5. Adjust height and weight as needed
  6. Always use a spotter for heavy weights
